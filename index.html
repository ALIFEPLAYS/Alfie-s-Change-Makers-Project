The Climate clear project

Climate clear is a project that proposes a way for consumers to make more informed choices about the environmental impact of their shopping decisions.

With increasing conceren regarding the accelarating warming of our climate, more people have become interested and doing what they can to help.

What better place to do that than in their weekly shop?

On this website you will find information regarding:

- The motivation behind the project

- How the proposed label would function

- How the data for said label is gathered and displayed

- Obstacles which could hinder the effectivness of the labeling system

- A shopping optimiser in which you can use see to start making an impact today!
